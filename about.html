My Hobbies & Passions

Exploring the things that bring joy, creativity, and meaning to my life

Volunteering

Throughout high school, I dedicated over 300 hours to volunteering for different school and community events. It’s something I truly loved — from organizing fundraisers to helping during festivals, I found joy in giving back and connecting with others. Volunteering taught me the importance of leadership, empathy, and teamwork, and it continues to be something I deeply value.

Creating

Another hobby of mine is creating. Whenever I get the chance, I love making small crafts using things I find around the house. One of my favorite projects was making pipe cleaner flowers — and recently, I’ve been making lilies out of pipe cleaners! It’s a relaxing way to express my creativity and make something meaningful out of simple materials.

Cooking

Cooking is one of my favorite hobbies. I love experimenting with traditional dishes from my culture, but I also enjoy making simple comfort foods like grilled cheese with tomato soup. Cooking helps me unwind, explore new flavors, and connect with my family through shared recipes and traditions.
